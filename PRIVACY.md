- **Communication:** Send important updates, notifications
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, security incidents
- **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform
- **Compliance:** Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)
